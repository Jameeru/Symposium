About Us

Welcome to Aicon2k25, the premier technical symposium hosted by the Department of Information Technology at Misrimal Navajee Munoth Jain Engineering College. Scheduled for April 25, 2025, this event brings together passionate minds to celebrate technology, innovation, and creativity.
With a perfect blend of technical excellence and non-technical brilliance, AICON_2K25 offers a platform for students to compete, collaborate, and conquer challenges that test their skills in various domains. The symposium features six technical and six non-technical events, carefully curated to inspire innovation and encourage participation. AICON_2K25 is not just about competition; it’s about learning, networking, and experiencing the thrill of problem-solving in a dynamic environment. Participants can look forward to an engaging mix of coding challenges, quizzes, strategic contests, and creative competitions, making it a truly memorable and rewarding experience. To make the event even more exciting. AICON_2K25 boasts a total cash prize worth ₹30,000, ensuring that talent and effort are well recognized and rewarded. Whether you are an aspiring tech enthusiast, a strategic thinker, or a creative mind, there’s something for everyone. Join us for a day filled with intellectual stimulation, thrilling competitions, and endless fun. Be a part of Aicon2k25 and make your mark in the world of technology!
